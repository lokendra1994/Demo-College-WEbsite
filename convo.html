First Annual Convocation-2012


  1. The First Convocation of Jaypee University of Engineering & Technology, Guna was held on 04 May 12. Hon’ble Shri Shivraj Singh Chouhan, Chief Minister Madhya Pradesh, Hon’ble Shri Digvijaya Singh, former Chief Minister, Madhya Pradesh and Hon’ble Shri K.L. Agarwal, Minister of General Administration, Narmada Valley Development & Aviation of Madhya Pradesh graced the occasion.
  2. The function was presided over by Hon’ble Shri Jaiprakash Gaur, the Chancellor and also attended by Shri Manoj Gaur; Managing Trustee Jaiprakash Sewa Sansthan & Member Governing Body of the University & Shri Sunny Gaur Hon’ble MD (Cements) and Member Governing Body.
  3. In the convocation following degrees were awarded:-
    Ph.D.- 5
    M.Tech- 4
    B.Tech- 364
    Diploma- 31
  4. Following students were awarded gold medals:-

    Chancellor's Medal:
    07095G PRATEEK KATIYAR ECE 9.4 CGPA

    Vice Chancellor's Medal:
    07068G MADHUR SRIVASTAVA ECE 9.3 CGPA
    07311G PRIYANKA AGRAWAL CSE 9.4 CGPA
    07403G ANANYA SAXENA CHE 9.3 CGPA
    07523G S. PRASANNA CE 9.1 CGPA



  5. Students’ Magazine Vartika was released by Hon’ble Chief Minister on the occasion.

  6. The convocation was preceded by inauguration of the University by Hon’ble Shri Shivraj Singh Chouhan, Chief Minister, Madhya Pradesh.

  7. To mark the occasion a grand musical evening was held on 03 May 12 in which Shri Udit Narain, Smt. Deepa Narain and entourage presented a musical extravaganza.
